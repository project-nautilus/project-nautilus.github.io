Kick Off!

By Cristian Barrué in meeting

December 10, 2021

On the 3th of February 2021, the kick-off meeting of the NAUTILUS project took place at the premises of the Fundació Joan Costa Roma in Consorci Sanitari de Terrassa. Researchers from the different consortium members and supporting clinical centers met to kick off the project and discuss the study to be performed and agree on the different protocols and tasks. This meeting will guide the research actions in the coming months and support selecting participants in the study tests1.

Nautilus at Salut Mental 360

By Cristian Barrué in PRESS

Mar Ariza and Neus Cano researchers of C3-CST group have contributed to Salut Mental 360 portal with a blog post about Nautilus project. The blog post discusses the effects of COVID-19 on the brain, including potential cognitive and mental health issues. It also highlights the importance of research in this area, as well as the need for further understanding of the long-term consequences of COVID-19 on the brain and mental health.
