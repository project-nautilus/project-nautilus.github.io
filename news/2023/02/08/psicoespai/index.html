Nautilus podcast at PsicoEspai

By Cristian Barrué February 8, 2023

The Official College of Psychology in Lleida podcast PsicoEspai has published a podcast where Nautilus has been presented. The Cognitive Disorders Unit of Lleida, at the University Hospital of Santa Maria de Lleida, is one of the 24 centers that are part of the NAUTILUS project to promote an artificial intelligence platform to study the cognitive sequelae of people who have suffered from COVID-19. “Cognition, emotion/behavior, functionality and brain connectivity in patients recovered from COVID-19 (NAUTILUS)” is the name of the project led by the Consorci Sanitari de Terrassa, the University of Barcelona (UB) and the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC ), which was funded in the last call for the Marathon (among other grants). The clinical psychologist and neuropsychologist of the University Hospital of Santa Maria and researcher of the Clinical Neurosciences research group of the Biomedical Research Institute of Lleida (IRBLleida), Dra. Anna Carnes Vendrell, coordinates the participation of the province of Lleida in the project.

The podcast has been presented at Segre newspaper.
