Nautilus at CaixaForum Madrid

By Cristian Barrué November 17, 2022

Maite Garolera has presented Nautilus at CaixaForum Madrid in the workshop Neurological Effects and Neuropsychological Alterations due to COVID-19. Garolera presented Nautilus with the mission to try to understand what happens with cognition and how covid affects the central nervous system. Preliminary results from observational studies indicate that people who have been in the ICU have more serious cognitive problems. Regarding the affected domains, executive function, information processing speed and social cognition stand out above all. To alleviate these effects, interventions are being carried out in Terrassa, such as the RehabCOVID Project, which, based on the use of augmented virtual reality, helps improve cognitive and emotional symptoms in patients with persistent covid. This workshop has been disseminated through other media like InfoSalus and IMMÉDICOportals.
