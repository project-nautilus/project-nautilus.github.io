Kick Off!

By Cristian Barrué in meeting

December 10, 2021

On the Xth of February 2021, the kick-off meeting of the NAUTILUS project took place at the premises of the Fundació Joan Costa Roma in Consorci Sanitari de Terrassa. Researchers from the different consortium members and supporting clinical centers met to kick off the project and discuss the study to be performed and agree on the different protocols and tasks. This meeting will guide the research actions in the coming months and support selecting participants in the study tests1.
